How It Works

Level in four steps.

From pulling in to perfectly level — faster than unpacking your camping chairs.

1

Set Up Your Profile

Enter your vehicle type, measurements, and the blocks you carry. Takes two minutes, once.

2

Place Your Phone

Set your phone on a level surface inside your vehicle and run Quick Calibrate.

3

Get Your Plan

Tap Leveling Assistant. FlatFinder reads the slope and tells you exactly what to stack and where.

4

Drive On & Confirm

Pull onto your blocks while listening for the beeps. Go green. Check Level. Enjoy your site.
