🎨 Design Specifications

  • Background: Radial gradient #1a1a2e → #0f172a
  • Main Element: Bubble level with glassmorphic effect
  • Crosshair: Dashed lines with subtle white/gray rgba(148,163,184,0.4)
  • Bubble Color: Green gradient #4ade80 → #22c55e → #16a34a
  • Precision Rings: Purple → Blue → Green (outer to inner)
  • Glow Effect: Green radial glow when level achieved
  • Glass Effect: Frosted appearance matching app design

✅ Why This Design Works

  • Instant Recognition: Bubble level is universally understood
  • Brand Alignment: Matches glassmorphic UI in the app
  • Findability: Crosshair communicates "finder" concept
  • Scalability: Clear and recognizable from 16px to 1024px
  • Premium Feel: Professional gradient and glass effects
  • Color Psychology: Green = level/success, aligned with app UX
